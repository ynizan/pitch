The market

The outreach arms race

  • The average inbox receives 121 emails per day — up 8.5% in two years
  • Cold email reply rates dropped from 7% to 5.1% in one year
  • Email unsubscribe rate tripled in 12 months due to Gmail changes
  • 73% of B2B buyers actively avoid automated outreach
  • B2B customer acquisition cost rose 40% in two years (222% in eight years)
Small teams can no longer hustle their way through cold outreach. They have to rely on who they know.
Sources: Statista/Radicati, MailerLite (3M+ campaigns), Gartner 2024, Phoenix Strategy Group
