The Networkers

338 million run on who they know

  • 82% of small businesses say referrals are their primary source of new customers
  • Small business owners are 2× more active on LinkedIn than average
  • 81% already spend 1+ hours/week trying to scale their network — and get poor results
The Math
16% at firms under 20 (Census) + 10% self-employed (BLS) = 26% × 1.3B professionals (LinkedIn) = 338M
338 million professionals can't scale their primary source of business.
Sources: Firework/industry data, LinkedIn SMB report, InTouch interviews (n=26), US Census Bureau, BLS
